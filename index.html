AOC Turns Heads, Gives Us Whiplash!

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, in the flesh. One known and beloved by many. This look, however? Purely and utterly shameful. Embarrassing, horrendous, horrific; the list goes on. We may all be thinking, "AOC, girl, what was the goal here?" This dress was designed by Brooklyn-based designer brand Brother Vellies, and let us just say they are irrelevant for a reason or two. If the dress was nice-looking, it would have potentially made up for the monstrosity that is the full piece.
